More capabilities. Faster decisions. Better control over production.

OmniMES is entering a new level — the latest version of the system has been designed to help your teams work faster, more efficiently, and with full access to the data they need at critical moments.

The refreshed, intuitive interface organizes key information and eliminates unnecessary steps — bringing you exactly where you need to be, right away.

**What do you gain with the new version?**

- Measurement comparison tool in the Utilities module — instant analysis of changes and deviations before they become an issue
- Expanded Planogram — clear presentation of statuses, sub-statuses, and production flows in one place
- Enhanced analytics and reporting components — decisions based on data, not assumptions
- Fully consistent interface — one platform, one experience, zero chaos

**Real value for the entire plant**

The new OmniMES is not just about a better look — it delivers tangible benefits for every department:

- Maintenance — faster access to operational data and measurement history
- Production — clearer views and more efficient status tracking
- Management — a complete real-time overview of the situation
